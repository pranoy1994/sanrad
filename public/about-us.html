The Group

Rich in heritage, the field of medicine is deeply ingrained in Sanrad’s roots as we complete twenty-five (25) years of service to the community in delivering state-of-the-art diagnostic imaging equipment (CT & MRI) at competitive rates.

Our strength lies in the way Sanrad is structured as a 21st-century, ethical engineering services company. We nurture and cultivate relationships in the medical community from the grassroots level to understand and therefore deliver on modern imaging equipment requirements to various medical facilities across India.

We are adept at walking you through the intricacies of not only buying technologically-advanced equipment from us but also provide industry-leading after-sales support services to the whole buying experience. With over five-hundred (500) successful installations, ten (10) support offices and hundred (100) employees across the country, Sanrad is committed to paving the way for the next wave of medical revolution in the country.

Our Leadership
Ratish S Nair (CEO)

The man behind the helm, Mr Ratish Nair has been a vocal proponent and the driving force behind the success of Sanrad Medical Systems in the country.

A team leader in his own stride, he brings his vast experience of over 25 years as an Electronics Engineer with stints at Toshiba Medical Equipments and United Group to practice and has been instrumental in the development and growth of the company.

Based in Mumbai, Ratish is presently involved in the expansion of Sanrad’s footprint both in India and abroad, and actively partakes in the firm’s growth strategy and business development initiatives.

Gayathri Vishweshwaraiah (Director)

Hailing from Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India, Gayathri Vishweshwaraiah is the Director for Sanrad and is responsible for the company’s growth and performance.

Possessing an Electrical Engineering background, she previously served roles as a Developer, Project Manager and EDP Engineer in a career spanning twenty-seven (27) years. She is best known for her work at the prestigious Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore in the areas of Renewable Energy and Aerospace.

She was also honoured with a Fellowship by the Government of India for her work on the “Waste to Energy” programme in conjunction with the Kingdom of Denmark.

Our Storied History

Every journey starts with a single step, and the founders of Sanrad long realised the immense potential and impact of electronics in the field of medicine especially in India.

Starting out as a pure-play aftercare services company in the summer of 1984, Sanrad three decades hence has established itself as a market leader in offering import and export services along with stellar sales and support services for authentic, pre-owned diagnostic imaging equipment from market leaders like Toshiba and Sina Healthcare.

Our Fabled Partner — Toshiba

Together, Everyone Achieves More.

This is the foundational imprint on which Sanrad has cultivated, fostered and developed integral partner relationships through the years. When it comes to a technology and support partner, commitment is vital, and we’ve been able to nurture a special relationship with Toshiba Medical Systems Corporation, a global leader since the 90s, specialised in the manufacture of diagnostic imaging systems that improves the quality of patient care, everywhere.

Our mutual philosophy is in having the “patient” at the heart in everything we do and ably assisting the community at large in contributing to the health and well-being of patients across the country through specialised products and customer-centric services from our chosen partners.

We are also proud to be distinguished as a trusted partner for Toshiba and currently house the most extensive inventory for CT Scanner spares for the company’s products both in India and abroad.
